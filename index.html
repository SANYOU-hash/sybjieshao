About Us

SYB Club was founded in 2020, dedicated to providing a platform for game enthusiasts and aviation enthusiasts to exchange, learn, and grow. Our vision is to help members improve their skills, broaden their horizons, and achieve excellence in their respective fields through organizing various activities, competitions, and training courses.

We focus not only on competitive results but also on team collaboration and personal development. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced player, SYB Club welcomes your participation. Here, you will meet like-minded friends and explore endless possibilities in e-sports and aviation together.

The club regularly holds online and offline gatherings and seminars, inviting experts and famous players in the industry to share and communicate. We also provide professional training facilities and technical support to ensure that every member can have the best learning experience.

PUBG Division [Enjoy the fun of gaming together]

In the PUBG division of SYB Club, we believe that gaming is not just competition but also enjoyment. Here, a group of people who love PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds (PUBG) gather to share the joy brought by the game.

No matter if you are a newcomer just starting out with this game or an old player with some experience, we welcome your joining. Our motto is "Easy Gaming, Happy Making Friends," where there are no strict technical requirements and no high-intensity competition pressure.

We organize several online gatherings every week, allowing you to form teams, parachute together, collect supplies, and fight against enemies. We encourage interactions and exchanges among members, sharing funny stories and experiences from the game. If you encounter any problems or need help, there are always enthusiastic teammates willing to lend a hand.

In addition to daily gaming activities, we occasionally hold interesting theme events such as "Double Points Day" and "Special Task Challenges." These activities not only add more fun to the game but also provide more opportunities for interaction among members.

By joining the PUBG division of SYB Club, you can not only experience exciting gameplay processes but also make many like-minded friends. Let's fight side by side on the virtual battlefield and enjoy the fun of each game!

Simulation Flight Division [Dream Sky]

For those passionate about aviation, the simulation flight division of SYB Club is an ideal place.

From basic principles of flying to complex operations under meteorological conditions, we will guide you step-by-step to master flying skills. In addition, we regularly organize simulation flight competitions and aerial exploration activities, allowing you to improve yourself in practice.

Our simulation flight division not only provides theoretical knowledge instruction but also includes practical operation training. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced pilot, you can find suitable activities here.

Skyward Virtual Airlines [Link Dreams]

Skyward Virtual Airlines is an important part of SYB Club, focusing on simulating airline operations and management. Here, you can experience roles ranging from pilots to cabin crew, understanding every detail of airline operations.

We offer not only flight simulations but also training in areas such as flight scheduling, customer service, safety management, and marketing. For more information about Skyward Virtual Airlines, please visit: Skyward Virtual Airlines Official Website
